FALCON 9

Falcon 9 is a two-stage rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transport of satellites and the Dragon spacecraft into orbit

DRAGON SPACECRAFT

Dragon cargo in the spacecraft's pressurised capsule and unpressurized trunk, which can also accomudate secondary payloads. In the future, Dragon will carry astronauts in the pressurised capsule as well.

COMPOSITE FAIRING

The payload firing is for the delivery of satellites to destination in low Earth orbit (LEO) geosynchronous transfer orbit(GTO) and beyond.

FIRST STAGE

Falcon 9's first-stage incorporations nine merlin engines and aluminium-lithium alloy tanks containing liquid oxygen and rocket-grade kerosene (RP-1) propellant.

NINE MERLIN ENGINES

With its nine first-stage Merlin Engine clustered together, Falcon 9 can sustain up to two engines shutdowns during flight and still successfully complete its mission. Falcon 9 is the only launch vehicle in its class with this key reliability feature.
